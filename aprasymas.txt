This mod makes minor, but significant changes to some of parameters. It changes starting capacity limits and alters maps (medium, large and huge) to support more empires.
• Changes in strategic resources (00_strategic_resources.txt): 
 Max resources are set to 500k;
 Influence capacity - 2k;
• Adds custom maps:
 "Huge (mod)" map allows to spawn up to 99 empires, up to 5 FE;
 "Large (mod)" map allows to spawn up to 59 empires, up to 5 (4 recommended) FE;
 "Medium (mod)" map allows to spawn up to 39 empires, up to 5 (3 recommended) FE.
